Dark Souls 1 overview

Before the begining, part 1: The age of Ancients

In the begining the world was cast in grey, not light nor dark, not life nor death. Immortal dragons made of stone ruled the surface wether they could move or not. Eventually, deep underground, a flame was lit by semingly nothing, and within it four powerful souls where found by the ancient, slumbering pygmys.

The four souls where the soul of life, death, light, and dark. The soul of life was found by the one who became known as the Witch Izalith and gave her power over flames. The soul of death was found by he who became Lord Nito, the first of the dead and gave him powers over all things dead. The soul of light was found by the one that became Lord Gwyn, the lord of sunlight and granted him power from the new born sun. And lastly the soul of dark which was found by the easily forgotten furtive pygmy who laid low and slowly split his soul into fragments which where spread amongst the humans.


Part 2: The war aginst dragons


Once the four souls had been collected, Gwyn wanted to take over the surface by slaying the dragons. In Gwyn's crusade against the dragons where at first futile, however he soon learned that his Lord Soul obtained from the fire allowed him to throw spears of lightning made from the power of the sun which could chip away at the dragon's stone scales thus exposing their flesh underneath.



Once some of the scales where removed from the dragons, Nito could use his Lord Soul to release a miasma of death and disease to lay the dragons to rest forever. While this was happening, the Witch Izalith together with her daughters burned the Archtrees that the dragons lived in to not give them anywhere to flee.
